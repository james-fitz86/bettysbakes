Banana Bread

The following recipe is for a rectangular tray or tin of size 22cm x12cm. To adjust the recipe to suit the size of your tray or tin, please enter the dimensions in the boxes below and scale the recipe to meet your needs!

Ingredients

  • 250 g ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 100 g granulated sugar
  • 100 g unsalted butter (melted)
  • 50 g brown sugar
  • 8 g baking powder
  • 4 g baking soda
  • 2 g salt
  • 5 g ground cinnamon
  • 2 g vanilla extract
  • 100 g eggs (about 2 medium eggs)
  • 50 g milk
  • 75 g chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 75 g chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 175°C and grease a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth.
  3. Whisk in the melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, then mix until smooth.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring gently until just combined. Do not overmix.
  7. Fold in the chopped walnuts and chocolate chips if using.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly.
  9. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Slice and enjoy!
